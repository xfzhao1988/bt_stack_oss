 ble_freertos_ancs


Description:
============
 ARM Cordio BLE - Apple Notification Center Service (ANCS) Example.




Purpose:
========
This example implements a BLE Apple Notification Center Service
 profile.

 To begin, after the Ambiq device is recognized by the Apple Device's BT,
 the user should pair in the normal bluetooth section of the phone. Once
 the pairing with the Apple Device is completed, the Apple Device
 notifications will be displayed in the ITM output.

 Printing takes place over the ITM at 1M Baud.
